You can access the Settings page by clicking on the "Help / Settings" button on the bottom menu of any page in the ISS FIT application:

Alternatively, you may also click on the "Help / Settings" button on the Login page:

Once you're on the Help / Settings page make sure to select the "Settings" tab:

The Settings page will be displayed:

To view or change your application settings, simply click on any of the settings categories listed on the left side of the screen. The settings for the selected category will be displayed on the right side of the screen. On next topic you can see the list of the available settings:
